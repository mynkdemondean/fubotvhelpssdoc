Contacting Fubo TV by Phone
---------------------------

Fubo TV provides phone support for customers needing direct assistance. Follow these steps:

1. Visit the official Fubo TV website: `https://www.fubo.tv`_.
2. Navigate to the **Help / Contact Us** section.  
3. Find the **Customer Service Phone Number**.  
4. Call during business hours for account, billing, or technical support.  

**Tips for Phone Support:**

- Keep your account details handy (email, subscription plan, last payment).  
- Note down reference numbers given during the call for future follow-up.  
- Be prepared to verify identity to access account information.

By following these steps, most Fubo TV issues can be resolved quickly without prolonged downtime.
